Why I Care

Opaltrix was a small business I started in 2020. Over quarantine, I started thrifting old clothing pitems, and started revamped them into trendier items by cutting uo different pieces and sewing them together. I think it’s so cool to give a worn out item a new life in someone else’s closet - while leaving the earth a little greener too.
