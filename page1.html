Tips on Shopping Sustainably

Retail Therapy You Can Feel Good About... What's Better Than That?

Shop Sustainable Brands.

Choose ethical brands that care about the environment, pay their workers fairly, and are transparent across their entire supply chain â€“ not just one part.

Quality Over Quantity

Higher quality pieces often last longer. Check the workmanship of your clothing.

Look for Certifications

It is important to keep an eye out for certifications which prove that clothes are made sustainably and ethically.

Shop Locally

Buy from local shops for clothes, buy from local craftspeople.

Go Natural

Buy clothing made with natural fabric fibers instead of synthetic fabrics. Natural alternatives to synthetic fabrics include cotton, linen, bamboo, flax, jute, silk, wool, and alpaca.

Buy organic

Buy clothing made with organic fabrics when you can.

Shop Secondhand First

Buy pre-owned items from thrift stores, consignment/vintage shops, and online resale shops.

Buy Fewer Clothes

Value what you buy. Buy clothes you know you will wear many times because they look good and fit well.

Staple Pieces

Invest only in clothes you really like and need. Before you buy a new piece of clothing, consider if you have a similar one in your wardrobe already.
